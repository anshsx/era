To install Arc Search, visit the app store on your device, search for "Arc Search" and click on install.
Arc Search is supported on Android and iOS devices, along with desktop operating systems such as Windows and macOS.
Go to your phone settings, navigate to the browser settings, and select Arc Search as the default browser.
Sign in with the same account on both devices, and your data will sync automatically across mobile and desktop.
You can share feedback via the feedback section in the app settings or by contacting the support team directly.
