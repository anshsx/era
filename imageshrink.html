How to Use

  1. Upload Your Image

    Click the upload area or drag and drop your image file. We support PNG, JPG, and GIF formats up to 5MB in size.

  2. Adjust Image Settings

    Enter your desired width and height, choose the resize method, and set the quality. You can also select the output format and whether to maintain the aspect ratio.

  3. Preview and Download

    Preview your changes in real-time. Once you're satisfied, click the download button to save your optimized image.

Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

Our tool supports PNG, JPG, and GIF formats for input. You can choose to output your image as JPEG, PNG, or WebP.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, the maximum file size for upload is 5MB. This limit helps ensure fast processing times.

What's the difference between crop and stretch?

Crop will maintain the image's aspect ratio by cutting off parts of the image that don't fit. Stretch will force the image to fit the new dimensions, which may distort the image.

Is my image data safe?

Yes, all image processing is done locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your data remains private and secure.
